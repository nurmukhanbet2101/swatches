Sign in

Learn how to order step by step

    Sign up

  1. Step one. You need to register, so we will find out where and to whom to send.
  2. Step two. You need to check your email that you used in registration page. And then confirm your account by clicking to link.
  3. Step three. Now you need login your data. Just click sign in.

Congratulation! You can move to the next article.

    Making an order

  1. After selecting the product you like, click the "Add to cart" button, and the product will automatically go to your cart.
  2. Then you can continue to select products in the online store, adding as many products to the basket as you need.
  3. If you have finished selecting products, proceed to checkout.

    Payment for the products

  • You can pay for purchases in the Technodom online store in cash or by bank transfer, including plastic cards: online (directly on the website) or upon receipt of the products.
